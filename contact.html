Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I specialize in web development including frontend development with React/Vue.js, backend development with Node.js/Laravel, and full-stack web applications. I also provide technical consultation and code review services.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and requirements. Simple websites typically take 1-2 weeks, while complex web applications can take 4-8 weeks. I always provide detailed timeline estimates after understanding your specific needs.

How do you structure your pricing?

I offer both fixed-price and hourly billing depending on the project. For well-defined projects, I prefer fixed pricing. For ongoing work or projects with evolving requirements, hourly billing works better. I always provide transparent pricing upfront.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and am comfortable with remote collaboration. I'm flexible with time zones and use modern communication tools to ensure smooth project coordination.

What technologies do you work with?

I primarily work with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Vue.js, Node.js, Laravel, MySQL, and PostgreSQL. I'm also experienced with modern development tools like Git, Docker, and various cloud platforms.

What's your project process?

I start with a discovery call to understand your requirements, followed by a detailed proposal. Once approved, I provide regular updates and demos throughout development. I ensure thorough testing before final delivery and offer post-launch support.
